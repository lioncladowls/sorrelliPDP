Cart Sidebar Redesign — Variant Comparison

Same data, 3 approaches to visual hierarchy and information density

A — Minimal

Gorjana-inspired. Strip to essentials. Brand is the trust signal. No savings box, no delivery, no promo.

B — Structured Sections

Same info, better grouping. Express checkout, order summary, and promo/gift in 3 separate cards.

C — Progressive Disclosure

Collapsed by default. Breakdown expands on click. Shortest initial height.

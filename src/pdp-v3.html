Every Sorrelli piece is designed to be worn and loved for years. A few small habits will keep yours brilliant for a long time to come.

Wearing

  • Put your jewelry on last, after hairspray, perfume, and lotions have had a moment to dry
  • Slip it off before showering, swimming, or exercising
  • Store pieces flat in their organza bag to keep crystals free from scratches
  • Set it down gently on soft surfaces to protect the crystal finish

Cleaning

  • A soft toothbrush with warm water and a touch of mild dish soap is all you need. Dry thoroughly right away.
  • Avoid jewelry baths, ultrasonic cleaners, and polishing cloths, which can dull the special coatings on our crystals
  • We also offer professional cleanings for $10 per piece (or per pair of earrings) if you'd prefer to leave it to us

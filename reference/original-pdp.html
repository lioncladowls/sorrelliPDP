Frequently Asked Questions

How is Sorrelli Different?+-

Sorrelli is small batch, and each piece of jewelry is delicately crafted in our studio by skilled artisans. That's very different from jewelry brands that make castings of hundreds of the same product at a time.

Our artisanal approach helps ensure durability, reduce waste, and deliver our signature look. We believe in responsible production. Our jewelry is made with care, for the people who made it, for the environment it was made in, and for you.

Do you use plastics?+-

No, we don't use plastics in our jewelry; just high quality crystal, genuine and dyed semi-precious stones, and natural materials (like pearls).

We also try very hard to minimize our use of plastics in our packaging and throughout our company. Why? Because it's good for the environment.

Do you have a satisfaction guarantee/money back guarantee?+-

Absolutely! We want you to love your new piece of Sorrelli. If for any reason you don't, simply ship it back to us within 30 days and we'll help you out with a quick exchange or refund. Certain restrictions and limitations apply.

How long does shipping take and what is the cost?+-

Our in-stock products usually ship the same day. If you order later in the day or on a weekend when we're closed we'll send your order out the next working day.

Most of our customers live within 1 to 3 days from our office in Kutztown in Pennsylvania, so product arrives pretty quickly by standard shipping. If you live on the U.S. west coast (California, etc.) or abroad, shipments can take 6 days or longer via standard shipping.

  • All orders over $130 (within the contiguous U.S.) ship free
  • International and rush shipping options vary by location
If you order a product that is out of stock, we will make it to order, though it will take extra time for us to make your product and then ship it to you.

What is your refund/exchange policy?+-

We want you to absolutely love your new piece of Sorrelli. If for any reason you don't, simply ship it back to us within 30 days and we'll help you out with a quick exchange or refund. Certain restrictions and limitations apply.

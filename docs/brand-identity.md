# Sorrelli Brand Identity

Distilled from the Image Gen Project's `config.json` — the prompts, scene directions, and creative guidelines that define how Sorrelli presents itself visually.

---

## Positioning

**Aspirational "achievable" luxury.** Not fine jewelry, not cheap costume — the sweet spot. Crystal-forward fashion jewelry with real design intent and craftsmanship feel. Sorrelli occupies the space where editorial quality meets accessible price points.

---

## The Sorrelli Woman

- Elegant, warm, feminine
- Lives in a tastefully decorated home — not a showroom, but *real* and curated
- Her spaces: the vanity, the bedside nightstand, the sunlit windowsill, the spa-like bathroom
- She treats her jewelry as personal, intimate objects — takes them off before bed, sets them on ceramic trays, catches them in the morning light
- She's confident but not showy. Her style says "I know what I like" without trying too hard

---

## Visual Language

### Lighting
- Always warm, natural, golden
- "Early afternoon sun streaming through a window"
- Warm but not orange, bright but not harsh
- Never cold, clinical, or blue-toned
- Evening scenes: "a beautiful bedside lamp mixed with the last glow of golden hour"

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Warm neutrals — cream, champagne, soft blush, warm white
- **Secondary:** Dusty rose, pale peach, soft mauve, pale sage
- **Acceptable accents:** Muted/desaturated jewel tones (soft lavender, dusty blue, muted plum) — always pastel-adjacent, never rich or deep
- **Internal shorthand:** "Hollywood Beige"
- **Never:** Deep burgundy, wine, navy, midnight blue, dark emerald, black, or any saturated jewel tone

### Surfaces & Materials
- Marble (Calacatta/Carrara) — subtle, organic veining
- Refined linen — high-thread-count, smooth, champagne/cream tones
- Velvet — soft nap, muted feminine tones (dusty rose, blush, champagne)
- Polished wood — warm, quality, not glossy or rustic
- Matte ceramic — simple, elegant shapes in soft neutrals
- Premium bathroom surfaces — spa-luxury, bright and clean

### Photography Mood
- Editorial but approachable
- Aspirational but lived-in
- "A beautiful, curated moment" — not a sterile catalog shot
- "A quiet, beautiful moment in an elegant woman's home"
- Personal and intimate, never corporate or cold

---

## What The Brand Is NOT

- Not cold / clinical / minimalist
- Not bold / saturated / dramatic
- Not rustic or weathered
- Not high-gloss showroom
- Not fine jewelry pretending — honest about its costume/fashion construction
- Not catalog-sterile or stock-photo generic

---

## Aesthetic Principles

### Composition
- Product is always the hero — 40-65% of frame, centered, razor-sharp
- Shallow depth of field on lifestyle/supporting elements
- Slightly elevated shooting angles (25-45 degrees) for product context
- Comfortable margins on all sides — breathing room

### Props & Styling
- Minimal: 1-2 supporting elements maximum
- Placed at frame edges, partially cropped, or softly blurred
- **Approved categories:**
  - Fresh greenery (eucalyptus, olive branch, monstera leaf)
  - Florals (single rose petal, scattered peony petals, dried lavender)
  - Vanity items (glass perfume bottle in pink/gold, small ceramic dish, satin ribbon)
  - Organic elements (single pearl, smooth river stone)
- Props must never touch, overlap, or crowd the product
- **Never:** Baby's breath, holiday items, candles with flames, food, branded items

### Light & Color Treatment
- Warm, golden undertones in everything
- Metals glow warmly
- Crystals catch and scatter light naturally — visible refraction and sparkle
- No cool or blue color casts, ever

### Lifestyle Scenes
- Vanity / dressing table — intimate, feminine, quietly luxurious
- Bedside nightstand — warm evening wind-down moment
- Bathroom counter — bright, spa-luxury morning routine
- Sunlit windowsill — optimistic, airy, luminous
- Each scene should feel like a *different woman's home* — varied but always on-brand

## Product Categories

- Rings
- Earrings (studs, drops, hoops, chandeliers, lever-backs)
- Necklaces
- Bracelets
- Cuff Bracelets

### Construction Reality
These are costume/fashion jewelry pieces. Settings, bezels, and prong plates are made from flat stamped brass sheet — shaped and folded, not thick cast metal. This is an important brand truth: Sorrelli doesn't pretend to be fine jewelry. The design and crystal quality speak for themselves.
